Privacy Policy for ORYN Chronoguard

Last updated: November 23, 2025

ORYN Chronoguard is developed by Blue Lattice Software. This privacy policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how your data is protected. Transparency and safety are core principles of this product.

1. Information We Collect

ORYN Chronoguard collects only the minimum data required for parental control and PIN recovery:

  • Parent PIN — stored locally on the child’s device, encrypted.
  • Recovery Email — stored securely in Firebase Firestore to send recovery codes.
  • Offline Recovery Code — generated and stored only on the device.
  • Device App List — used locally to determine which apps are blocked during sessions.
  • App usage events — processed locally through Android’s AccessibilityService.

No browsing history, content, messages, photos, or keystrokes are ever collected.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • PIN Recovery: If you forget your PIN, we email a one-time recovery code.
  • Enforcing App Locks: The Accessibility API is used only to detect which app is currently open so Chronoguard can apply your blocklist rules.
  • Error Diagnostics (Opt-in only): Anonymous crash data may be collected if you enable it.

3. What We Do Not Collect

ORYN Chronoguard does not collect or transmit:

  • Personal device content
  • Passwords, PINs, or biometrics
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers outside of Google Ads
  • Children’s data for profiling
  • Any data for resale or sharing with third parties

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Firestore – stores recovery email and recovery metadata.
  • Firebase Cloud Functions – sends recovery emails and validates codes.
  • SendGrid – email delivery of PIN recovery codes only.
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK – displays non-personalized ads.

All services are used strictly for functionality, never for user profiling.

5. Non-Personalized Ads

ORYN Chronoguard displays non-personalized ads to comply with child safety requirements. Google may use cookies to limit repeat impressions, detect invalid traffic, or aggregate analytics, but no personalized targeting is used.

6. Data Storage and Security

All sensitive data (PIN, offline code) is stored only on the device. Email is stored securely in Firestore under encryption by Google Cloud.

7. Children’s Privacy

Chronoguard is a parental control tool installed by adults. It is not designed for use directly by children, and collects no personal data from children.

8. Your Choices

  • You may delete the app at any time.
  • You may clear your recovery email from the app’s settings.
  • You may request deletion of recovery email records by contacting: bluelatticesoftware@gmail.com.
